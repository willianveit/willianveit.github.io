Melist

Previous Next


    Melist is an application that helps people buy products at the best price in super markets.
    First, the user selects the products he wants to buy, and then the app shows all the super markets in the region with their respective prices.
    I built this app alone in 21 days (the layout was ready, it was designed by Édy Marcozzi), but the app does not communicate with any api/database. All of your data is static (dummy data).
    Unfortunately, the client who paid me to do the application depends on investors to proceed the next stage of the project. I hope to continue to build this application that has an incredible purpose.

Application for an accounting firm


    This was the biggest project I've ever worked on. A team was made up of 8 people (programmers, designers and domain experts).

    In this application, I worked on both the front-end and the back-end.

    In the Front-end, I built widgets, screens, animations and transitions.
    In the Back-end, I created business rules, built blocs and made requests via Rest (which interacted with the front-end, of course).

    I am not allowed to publicly display this app, however if you are interested, we can make a call and I can tell you more about this app.
